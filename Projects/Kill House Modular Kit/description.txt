Kill House Modular Kit
---
I wanted to actually get something fun done, so I challenged myself to make a killhouse using only one trimsheet for the modular bits and one for the decal atlas!
Rendered in Unity.
HDRI and ground texture are from polyhaven! https://polyhaven.com
